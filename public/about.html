About Me

I am a developer who has been building websites since 2021. I specialize in clean code, and a user-friendly web design, without the need to sacrifice creativity.

I discovered web development in my quest to learn more about cybersecurity, and I have been hooked ever since. I have learned the creative liberties you can take with web development and just how freeing and fulfilling it can be. From recreating the Star Wars opening crawl, to a website version of Tom Riddle's diary, creating websites has become not just a hobby, but a passion.

I graduated from The Ohio State University with a BA in English Literature, and I am currently pursuing a BS in Computer Science from Western Governors University. I specialize in front-end development and languages such as HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, etc.
